MIT Ridonkulous
MIT Ridonkulous was originally founded in Spring 2005 by a group of committed dancers at MIT. In Fall 2006, a group of us got Ridonkulous started up again to bring dance at MIT to other schools and venues in the Greater Boston area.
 
Past performances have included charity events and college shows at BU, Babson, and Wellesley, as well as MIT events such as Comedy Collage and AXO Lip Sync.
 
We look forward to networking and groovin with other dancers. Most importantly we are here to have fun and to dance.
