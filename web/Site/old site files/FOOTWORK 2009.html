Ladies of Essence.
Founded in November 2005 by Gigi Torres after a troubling period in her life.  These moments bonded her closer to good friends and good times.  They decided to go forth with the group and share a piece of themselves and their lives with others telling their stories through the stage. The Ladies of Essence are pursuing their individual goals of the entertainment industry while using a sisterhood of support, love, and friendship. Women of different backgrounds. Women of different personalities. But ALL share the same passion for living life to the fullest.
 
The Essence Mission is to inspire those out there no matter what color, gender, and age to go ahead and do what you love to do whether it be dance or any other goal. Pursue your dreams and aspirations with no fear with the vision of success and happiness.
 
Gigi Torres Workshop.
Gigi Torres is originally from Irvine, CA, she has been training in all areas of dance from the age of 3. She is an worldwide instructor, sharing her knowledge and love for dance from the East Coast to the West Coast and overseas in Europe, Asia, and Australia. Gigi's choreography credits are So You Think You Can Dance Scandinavia and Ukraine, Choreographer's Carnival Hollywood from 2005-present, Funkanometry, Breed OCLA, Kaba Modern, etc. She is the Director/Founder of the All-Female Dance Crew "Essence" that consists of ladies working in the entertainment industry and an Instructor/Choreographer for Dance 2XS. Represented by Bloc Talent Agency, some of her dance credits include Asia Entertainment San Jose, Pussycat Dolls, Snoop Dogg Live in Vegas, Mims, Lee Hom ft Jin's Music Video "Heroes of the Earth and " Michael Jordan Commercial's - "Second Generation" and "Fist." Gigi lives by the Essence Motto "Inspire and Be Inspired. Just Be…& Live."
