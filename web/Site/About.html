RiDONKULOUS

ABOUT//

MIT Ridonkulous was originally founded in Spring 2005 by a group of committed dancers at MIT. In Fall 2006, a group of us got Ridonkulous started up again to bring dance at MIT to other schools and venues in the Greater Boston area.  Since then, we have grown to a team of 20+ dancers and our impact has extended beyond the collegiate realm, gaining regional and national recognition as a competitive crew.


Past performances have included charity events and collegiate shows.  In Fall 2009, we competed in shows including BU Fusion’s Elements X and MIT’s Ring the Alarm, placing 3rd and 2nd respectively, amongst some of the East Coast’s best hip hop crews. Not to mention, we were quite successful during America’s Best Dance Crew Season 4 + 5 audition seasons!  Outside of competitions, we have also hosted a number of workshops, taught by our own members and some of the nation’s top choreographers.


We look forward to networking and grooving with other dancers. Most importantly, we are here to have fun, dance, and show the world why we are so RIDONKULOUS!
